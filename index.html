PIN-EVENT is the union of a calendar as Google Calendar and a compilation as Pinterest.

PIN-EVENT is born of the need to share in a social network all events, anniversary and those dates that are important for everyone.

From PIN-EVENT will see all your events per year, per month or per day and you can also wiew the of other users. You will be able to look for events for places, per dates, for topics, etc.

PIN-EVENT also have a social button which when reading an event you are interested in any web page it can automatically add your profile PIN-EVENT (courses, concerts, fairs ...)

PIN-EVENT meet all standards of accessibility and usability for everyone to join the network and also you can see it on your favorite device.
